Overview

Book a great value Milford Sound day tour from Queenstown and see one of the world's most spectacular natural attractions. Cruise the fiord and experience this dramatically beautiful landscape with its towering mountain peaks, gushing waterfalls, ink-dark water and sheer cliffs covered in lush native rainforest.

Dolphin Watching Cruise

Set off on a Bay of Island cruise, enjoying stunning views of the region's many islands as you head out to the Cape Breet Lighthouse and through the famous Hole in the Rock (conditions permitting). Throughout your cruise your skipper will keep an eye out for Dolphins and other marine life, and where possible get up close to the playful creatures.

Choose from 3 cruise options:

Choose from a range of Milford Sound day tour options to suit every price range. All tours include return travel from Queenstown to Milford Sound and back, in a glass roof coach* with free wifi and commentary from your driver guide, plus a Milford Sound cruise. All cruise options includes commentary on the natural history and wildlife of the area. All cruise boats have indoor and outdoor seating options allowing viewing and panoramic photo opportunities in all weather conditions.
