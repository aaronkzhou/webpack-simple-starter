Itinerary details

Your pass is divided into the following 'legs'. You can book travel in aither direction (i.e. follow the itinerary from top to bottom, or bottom to top). You can also stop as often as you like along the way on each leg for example, on the Christchurch to Queenstown leg you can book from Christchurch to Ashburton, Ashburton to Tekapo and Tekapo to Queenstown.

When booking tours you must book to and from the stops shown. View our TravelPass tours page for more information.
